Company Rules

The Dos & Don'ts.

Every team member, partner, and creator on our roster agrees to these. Violations are grounds for immediate termination of partnership.

  • Sign and honor the NDA before accessing any creator account.
  • Respect every creator's voice, brand, and stated boundaries.
  • Use secure passwords and two-factor authentication on all accounts.
  • Report suspicious behavior, scams, or platform violations immediately.

  • Stay current with platform terms (OnlyFans, Fansly, Fanvue, etc.).

The Don'ts

  • Never apply, work, or interact if you are under 18 — strictly enforced.
  • Never share creator content, identity, or login credentials.
  • Never engage in messaging that is illegal, exploitative, or violates platform rules.
  • Never reference real-world meetups, contact info, or off-platform exchanges.
  • Never use AI bots or auto-responders unless explicitly approved.
  • Never poach creators, chatters, or trade secrets to competitors.
  • Never solicit, accept, or engage with anyone who appears underage.
  • Never breach confidentiality, even after a partnership ends.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

VELURRA Agency ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

What we collect

  • Application data: Name, email, age confirmation, country, platform handles, and any answers you submit through our application forms.
  • Creator data: Public creator profiles, performance metrics, content guidelines, and brand-voice notes shared with us during onboarding.
  • Chatter data: Identity documents (for verification), tax/payment information (where applicable), training records, and performance data.
  • Technical data: Anonymized analytics, page visits, device type, and approximate location for site improvement.

How we use it

  • To review applications and onboard partners and team members.
  • To provide chat moderation services and produce performance reports.
  • To process payments and meet legal/tax obligations.
  • To communicate updates, opportunities, and important policy changes.

How we protect it

All data is stored encrypted, behind role-based access controls. Only verified, NDA-signed staff may view sensitive material, and only when required for their role. We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@velurraagency.com. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations.
